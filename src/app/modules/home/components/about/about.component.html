How Does Invent-C Work?

The process of LCI begins with its first component the "Problem Identification", under first WHAT question.

Why Question

Followed by WHY question with components, "Literature Search", "Existing Solutions", "Market Landscape" and "Novelty". The next WHAT question is then answered by a component "Research Question".

How Question

The HOW question is then answered by component, "Research Methodology", "Key Resources", "Funding" and "Team Capacities".

What Question

The WHAT question is then responded by a component, "Research Outcome".
